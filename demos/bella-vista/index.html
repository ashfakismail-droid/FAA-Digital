Our Story

A family kitchen, three generations in the making.

Bella Vista opened its doors in 2012 with a simple promise: every plate would taste like it came from a nonna's kitchen in Puglia. Fifteen years later, that promise hasn't changed — even as the menu has grown to celebrate the whole of Italy's coastline and countryside.

We import our '00 flour and San Marzano tomatoes directly from Campania, cure our own salumi in-house, and hand-roll every ball of dough on the day it's served. Nothing here is frozen, and nothing is rushed.

Meet the Chef

"Cooking Italian food isn't about following a recipe — it's about respecting where the ingredient came from."

Alessandro Moretti

Executive Head Chef

Trained in Bologna and seasoned across kitchens in Florence and Naples, Chef Alessandro leads our kitchen with an obsession for seasonal produce and technique passed down rather than invented. Every tasting menu begins with a walk through the morning market.
