Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with LUXORA.

How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking link. You can also view status under My Orders.
What is your return policy?
We offer complimentary returns within 30 days of delivery, provided items are unworn and in original packaging.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes. We ship worldwide with fully insured, signature-required delivery on all orders.
Are all products authentic?
Absolutely. Every piece is sourced directly from verified brands and ateliers and comes with an authenticity guarantee.
Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay and Cash on Delivery in selected regions.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. Contact our concierge for assistance.
How does the currency switcher work?
Prices are stored in USD and converted live to your selected currency (EUR, GBP, INR, AED, SAR, JPY). Your choice is saved automatically.
Do you offer gift wrapping?
Yes — every order arrives in complimentary signature LUXORA packaging, perfect for gifting.
