Our Story

A Gallery of Automotive Excellence

Founded in 1998 in the heart of Mayfair, Prestige Motors began with a single conviction: that acquiring a luxury vehicle should feel as exceptional as the car itself. What started as a boutique showroom has grown into one of the UK's most respected luxury dealerships.

We represent eighteen of the world's most prestigious marques and maintain a live inventory of over a hundred hand-selected vehicles — each inspected to a 150-point standard before it earns our floor.
